Dark Energy

TECH: Unity3D, UNet, procedural generation

Dark Energy was the first game I created with the intention of releasing. It's extremely nostalgic for me and represents mountains of learning as I transitioned from an academic programmer (creating mostly data analysis programs for astronomy and physics labs) to a game programmer.

The idea for this came from my love of action RPGs such as Torchlight and Path of Exile. The weapons system in Dark Energy was developed based on my infatuation with the weapons system in the Borderlands series.

Main features include:

  • Randomized maze-like maps.
  • Procedurally generated weapons.
  • Local area gameplay using the old version (now deprecated) of UNet.

One day I hope to go back and completely revamp Dark Energy with everything I have learned. It was far from perfect, but it captured much of the burning desire I had to create games since I was in elementary school.
