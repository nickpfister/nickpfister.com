Planetary Defense

TECH: Unity3D, ARCore, Photon Unity Networking (PUN)

Planetary Defense was the first AR title I released, and it was highly experimental. That being said, I think it held up well and I personally enjoy going back and playing it even today. This title had several features which made it stand out:

  • The player used their phone to "aim" and position their spaceship in the world.
  • Synchronous multiplayer gameplay created with Photon Unity Networking (PUN).
  • Rank and powerup systems encouraged players to keep playing and beat their high-scores.

Planetary Defense was developed with ARCore and released as a beta on the Google Play Store. If you have an android phone, you can download it here.
